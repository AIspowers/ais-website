Data Readiness Assessment

A focused, 2-week audit of your data systems, workflows, and AI readiness. We find out exactly where you stand — and give you a clear roadmap to move forward.

What Is This?

A comprehensive assessment where we examine your data infrastructure, workflows, and organizational readiness for AI adoption. Think of it as a full health check for your data — before you invest in AI tools that may not work with your current setup.

Who Is This For?

  • Mid-market companies (50-500 employees) running on multiple data systems
  • Organizations exploring AI but unsure where to start
  • Companies that have tried AI tools and hit walls
  • Leadership teams seeking data-backed AI investment decisions
Telecom & ISPs Financial Services B2B / SaaS Government
Week 1

Discovery & Audit

We map your entire data landscape — every system, every workflow, every pain point.

  • Stakeholder interviews — conversations with 5-8 key team members across departments
  • System inventory — complete mapping of all data sources, storage, and flow between systems
  • Data quality sampling — spot checks across critical data sets for completeness, consistency, and accuracy
  • Process documentation — how data currently moves through your organization
Week 2

Analysis & Recommendations

We analyze everything and build your custom AI readiness roadmap.

  • AI readiness scoring — each system and data source rated on a clear framework
  • Gap analysis — what needs to be fixed before AI can deliver real value
  • Quick wins identification — high-impact, low-effort opportunities to start immediately
  • Investment modeling — expected ROI for recommended AI implementations
What You Get

Assessment Report

20-30 page comprehensive document covering:

  • Data quality scores per system
  • Integration gap analysis
  • AI readiness heat map
  • Risk assessment

Implementation Roadmap

Phase-by-phase plan with clear priorities:

  • Quick wins (0-3 months)
  • Foundation work (3-6 months)
  • Advanced AI capabilities (6-12 months)

Business Case Document

Board-ready materials including:

  • Expected ROI calculations
  • Resource requirements
  • Risk mitigation strategies

60-Minute Presentation

Live walkthrough with your leadership:

  • Key findings and recommendations
  • Q&A with our team
  • Decision framework for next steps

What We Don't Do in This Engagement

Transparency matters. Here's what's outside the scope of the assessment:

  • We don't build anything — this is assessment only
  • We don't access sensitive customer or employee data
  • We don't require changes to your current systems
  • We don't lock you into a long-term contract
